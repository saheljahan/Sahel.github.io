🔥 DESeq2 Tip: Compare Timepoints Correctly

Use interaction terms instead of naive comparisons:

design = ~ condition + time + condition:time
dds <- DESeq(dds)
results(dds, name="conditionTreatment.timeD4")

Why it matters: avoids incorrect conclusions in time-course data.

📊 Python: Quick Pandas Trick

df.groupby("gene")["expression"].mean().sort_values()

Fast way to summarize expression across groups.
