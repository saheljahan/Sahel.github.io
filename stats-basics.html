Two-sample t-test

What it is: compares the means of two independent groups.

Best for: treatment vs control, or any comparison of two separate groups.

Paired t-test

What it is: compares two related measurements.

Best for: before vs after measurements from the same samples.

FDR correction

What it is: adjusts many p-values to control false discoveries.

Best for: RNA-seq, omics, and multiple comparison analyses.
